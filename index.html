From these viewpoints and these news, let's talk about the robbery and destruction in the protest. I don't focus on the theft of large capital enterprise here, nor do I see the robbery from the perspective of 「White Man」or a「Neutral」,(If people really want to reckon, how many Native Americans and blacks souls and lands white people robbed?) but to look at the whole incident from the perspective of the protest itself. Therefore, the question lies in how we can explain the behavior of the protesters looting the small shops of ethnic minorities (such as Asians and Latinos). What's more, it also shows the protesters destroying the shelter of homelessness such as "the underprivileged Groups harm the other Underprivileged Groups " situation. If the Anarchism groups(such as "Antifa ") are to defeat any system of capital and authoritarianism, then why do they throw wolves to the proletariat? Furthermore, is there an internal contradiction in this Anarchism Group? Do they put themselves in a privileged position and reproduce another class after closing the gap between some class systems? For example, there was a black woman shouting at protesters loudly: "You are destroying the community here! You are destroying the people who live here! These people live in social houses and have to go by bus Work every day, go to the hospital every day, and those homeless people, you are destroying the person like this! " So, the problem with these violent demonstrations is that these things constantly intensify internal conflicts. The social system has to be changed, but the lives of more disadvantaged groups shouldn't be sacrificed at the same time. Although many people think that protesters are not equal to violents, and believe that violents cannot represent the position of the protesters, but I think these two are inseparable. There are always some of the protesters supporting the violent struggle. For example, in the Hong Kong anti-extradition, the valiant was throwing gasoline bombs at the police and smashing the police office building. In the Taiwan Sunflower Movement, the hawks also forcibly occupied the Executive Yuan. Even in the February 28 incident, the protester organized militia Army against the National Government. Furthermore, it has been found that speculators have involved in a lot of violent incidents frequently. Not only the extremism group "Antifa " which is listed as a terrorist group by Trump but 3K party (Ku Klux Klan) also mingled into the riot. The truth that the media did not show to people was that white people also robbed and seized the store, blurring the movement of black people as the main body. Therefore, in addition to resolving internal contradictions, but the protester also has to worry about intentional conflicts by speculators. To sum up, I think this protest obey the preamble in the United States Declaration of Independence which outlines a general philosophy of government that justifies revolution when government harms natural rights, like Frantz Fanon claimed when the colonists deal with the colonized through structural violence, the colonized must relatively use the armed revolution to destroy the colonial regime to eliminate the problem of colonialism causing spiritual disorder to the people, however, he did not mention effects and sequelae bring from the violent revolution. Thus, no matter what you are, the elephant or the jackass, people should focus on how to fix the problems among this kind of protest to avoid the situation being a vicious cycle.
